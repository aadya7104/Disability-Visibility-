Types of disabilities

  1. Physical Disability
    • Locomotor disability: Leprosy Cured Person, Cerebral Palsy, Dwarfism, Muscular Dystrophy, Acid Attack Victims
    • Visual Impairment: Blindness, Low vision
    • Hearing Impairment: Deafness, Hard of hearing
    • Speech or language disability

  2. Intellectual disability
    • Specific Learning disabilities
    • Autism Spectrum disorder

  3. Mental Illness

  4. Disability caused due to:
    • Chronic neurological conditions such as: Multiple Sclerosis, Parkinsons Disease.
    • Blood disorder: Thalassemia, Haemophilia, Sickle Cell Disease.

  5. Multiple disabilities
